New York City vs Chicago

New York and Chicago are 1st and 3rd highest populated cities of the USA. Both cities show a concentration of wealth in a close area, with crime being especially high in tourist locations, such as Times Square in New York and in 'bad reputation' neighborhoods, such as Fuller Park in Chicago. The higher income per capita of New York may cause the seemingly higher life expectancy of New Yorkers.

Created by Christian Ortiz, Richa Kulkarni, Ismael Lopez, Amita Sinha

in collaboration with Suresh K Lodha, Ryan Brounley, and Karthik Thota.

Description: This visualization provides a side-by-side comparison of Chicago and New York City. There are several variables used to compare the two such as: population, income, life expectancy, and crime. A slider tool provides a direct comparison between areas in Chicago and New York City for a selected range for each metric.
